About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Shruti Manilal Sharkar, a dedicated and creative web developer with a passion for crafting meaningful and efficient solutions. My journey in the world of technology began with a keen interest in building applications that make a positive impact on people's lives.

I hold a Diploma in Computer Engineering and am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in programming, algorithms, and software development methodologies.

In the realm of web development, I specialize in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy turning design ideas into interactive and responsive user interfaces. On the back-end, I have experience with server-side scripting and database management.

I have actively contributed to various projects, including a Women Safety Application developed for Android, a Resume Builder Web Application, and a landing page for PVG College. These projects showcase my ability to tackle diverse challenges and deliver high-quality solutions.

Outside of coding, I love attending tech meetups, participating in hackathons, and engaging in the tech community. My goal is to continue learning and growing as a developer, contributing to impactful projects, and making a positive mark in the ever-evolving world of technology.
