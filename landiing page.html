About Us

Welcome to Pune Vidyarthi Griha's College of Engineering (PVGCOE) and S.S. Dhamankar Institute of Management (SSDIM) in Nashik. We are committed to providing quality education and fostering an environment of academic excellence.

Departments

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering department focuses on cutting-edge technology and software development, preparing students for careers in software engineering and technology solutions.

Information Technology

The Information Technology department equips students with the skills to manage and implement information systems, preparing them for roles in IT management and consulting.

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

The AI and Data Science department focuses on the fields of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics, preparing students for roles in data-driven decision-making.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department provides a comprehensive understanding of mechanical systems and design, preparing students for careers in manufacturing, energy, and design engineering.

Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

The E&TC Engineering department covers a wide range of electronics and communication technologies, preparing students for roles in telecommunications, signal processing, and electronic systems.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To achieve excellence in engineering education with a focus on technical skills.

Mission

  • To achieve high standards of excellence in generating and propagating knowledge in engineering.
  • To impart knowledge and skill-based affordable education to all classes of society.
  • To fulfill the expectations of stakeholders by equipping students with state-of-the-art technological resources.
  • To provide an ecosystem for nurturing the spirit of entrepreneurship among students.
